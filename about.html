History of FlexCar

One night, in 2019, sitting in the kitchen in Athens, Anton Klymenko and Oleg Sergeev decided to call their former classmate at California State University, Ernest Halimov. He lived for several years in Milan, where in recent years car sharing has started to develop very rapidly. Inspired by the experience of Milan, they decided to try to do it in Athens. Athens, at that time, was suffocating from traffic jams, and paid parking had just begun to appear.

They were convinced that the idea would take root in Athens and would have a beneficial effect on the transport environment, as the average Athenian used his personal car only 4% of the time, and 96% of the car was idle with dead weight, but at the same time 600 new cars appeared every day on Athensâ€™s crowded roads cars. It became clear: this problem should have some kind of solution, it's time for car sharing.

But the dream was destined to come true only at the end of 2019, when it became possible to finance the project.

About FlexCar

FlexCar is a company that provides a service that allows people to find affordable cars near them and rent them through a mobile application, and very quickly, conveniently and inexpensively. The company was founded in 2019 by Oleg Sergeev, Ernest Halimov and Anton Klymenko, who together received an MBA from California State University. From the first day the service was launched, the company exhibited 100 cars in Athens.

The service works according to the all-inclusive concept, which means that parking, gasoline and insurance are already included in the price of the service. FlexCar works with both private and corporate clients, and also pays great attention to working with universities.

The main value of the company is technological effectiveness and openness, therefore the company has a young and ambitious team, the average age of the employee is 25 years.

Own IT platform allows you to provide quality service and quickly respond to customer feedback.
